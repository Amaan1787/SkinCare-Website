About Us

Dr. Sai Pawar

Dr. Sai Pawar, a renowned dermatologist, holds an MBBS and MD in Dermatology, Venereology, and Leprosy from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences. Her rigorous training under some of the country's leading dermatologists has ingrained a deep-rooted commitment to ethics, compassionate care, and excellence.

With over 8 years of diverse experience in both government and private healthcare sectors, Dr. Sai Pawar emphasizes attentive listening and personalized treatment strategies to effectively address complex skin, hair, and nail disorders across all age groups, including pediatric patients.

She has completed a fellowship in Aesthetic Medicine and is highly skilled in cutting-edge cosmetic procedures such as laser treatments (targeting pigmentation, scar revision, hair reduction, vascular lesions), neurotoxins, dermal fillers, skin tightening, and has performed numerous minor and major dermatologic surgeries.

Dr. Sai Pawar is also a published researcher and has presented her findings at national and international conferences. She is a proud recipient of a fellowship from the European Academy of Dermatology and Venereology (EADV) for her work in skin pigmentation.

As an active member of the Indian Association of Dermatologists, Venereologists, and Leprologists (IADVL) and the Association of Cutaneous Surgeons of India (ACSI), she remains committed to staying abreast of the latest advancements and innovations in dermatology.
